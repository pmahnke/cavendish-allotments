Froglife is a conservation and education charity. On Sunday 10<sup>th</sup> May 1-3pm on Cavendish House Allotment Site, Froglife will hold an interactive workshop for allotment holders.

The workshop offers in-depth discussion, tips and practical skills on how to make your allotment attractive to all sorts of wildlife, especially amphibians and reptiles.

h4. More Information

* All ages are welcome.
* Please email "enquiries@cavendish-allotments.org.uk":mailto:enquiries@cavendish-allotments.org.uk or phone 07505 668268 if you would like to attend.
* Only 15 places!
* More information can be found at "www.froglife.com":http://www.froglife.org
