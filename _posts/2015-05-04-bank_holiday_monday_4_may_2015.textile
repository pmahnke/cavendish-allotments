On Bank Holiday Monday, from 3pm to 5pm, join fellow plot holders for:

* enjoy tea and cakes
* swap Seeds
* exchange advice on Spring Gardening topics

Contributions for tea and cake will go towards communal projects such as raised beds and tree pruning.
