Richmond council are hoping to revive use of Moormead Pavilion as a community facility with public toilets.

I know this is a topic of interest to many of you!

There are 3 proposals on which they would like to hear your views before 3rd May:

* cafe
* sporting facility
* nursery

"Please submit your views online":https://consultation.richmond.gov.uk/parks-open-spaces/moormead
