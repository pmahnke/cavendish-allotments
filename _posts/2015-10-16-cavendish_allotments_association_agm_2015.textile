_Held on 29<sup>th</sup> November 2015 at St Stephen's School Hall from 2pm to 4pm._


h3. Agenda


* Welcome, Apologies & Introductions - Paul
* Minutes of the 2014 Cavendish Allotments AGM - Paul
* Chairman's Report including Best Plot & Best Newcomer's Plot - Paul
* Treasurer's Report - Rosemary
* Regular Features: Seed Scheme, compost, horse manure, wood chips, plot turnover
* Election of Committee Members
** The following are willing to continue - 
*** Paul Leonard (Chairman of meetings), Rosemary Fulljames (Treasurer),
*** Committee Members: Ann, Barbara, Barney, Christine, Harald, Yolanda.
*** There is one vacancy on the committee.
* BRAG (Borough of Richmond Allotment Group) update.
* Any other business.
* Close of meeting with refreshments.
