h4. Outdoors

February is your last chance to winter prune apple, pear and quince trees, red and white currants and gooseberry bushes.

If the soil doesn't stick to your spade, dig over beds and keep weeding.

Force rhubarb by covering the crown with straw and covering with an upturned bucket.
