Sunday 25 November at St Stephen's School, Winchester Road, St Margarets. Doors open 1400h, meeting from 1430h until 1530h.

Your opportunity to ask questions and hear what your committee has been doing.

Anyone unable to attend the AGM can e-mail ( "enquiries@cavendish-allotments.org.uk":mailto:enquiries@cavendish-allotments.org.uk )or text their question (07505 668268). Vote for your favourite plot. Bottle of wine for the plot with most votes. Refreshments available.
