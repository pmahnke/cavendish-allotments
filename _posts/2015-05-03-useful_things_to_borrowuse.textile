There are a few items that any plot-holder my borrow.  These include:

* Incinerator
* Sundry tools: spade, fork, rake, wheel barrow
* Bays available in the communal greenhouse (&pound;5 a bay for 12 months)

Please contact a committee member by leaving a message on  07505 668268, or email "enquiries@cavendish-allotments.org.uk":mailto:enquiries@cavendish-allotments.org.uk to arrange to use these items.
