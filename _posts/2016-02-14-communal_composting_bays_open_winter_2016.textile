Is your compost bin overflowing?

The communal composting bays will be open from 2-4pm on the following Saturdays and Sundays:

* February 20<sup>th</sup> and 21<sup>st</sup>
* March 5<sup>th</sup> and 6<sup>th</sup>

Come along on these dates to add material to the heaps or take away well rotted compost.

_It's free and ideal as a soil conditioner._
