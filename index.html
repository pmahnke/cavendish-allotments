Cavendish Allotment site is located between the A316, Hill View Road, Cole Park Road and the River Crane in St Margarets.

The allotment site is owned and managed by the London Borough of Richmond upon Thames with help from Cavendish House Allotment Holders Association committee.

See the LBRUT allotment webpage to find out:

  • how to apply for an allotment
  • request a replacement key
  • pay your allotment rent
  • see the terms and conditions

Visit the Richmond Council's allotment webpage ›

Cavendish House Allotment Holders Association committee show potential plot holders vacant plots and arrange social events and work parties for general site maintenance.

The committee is elected annually at the AGM which is usually held in November.
